Discover the beauty of our coast, the islands around us and enjoy in the adventure.

Adventure activites

If you want to feel some adrenaline or maybe a litlle bit of adventure we can recommend Rafting & Kayaking on the Cetina river, Paragliding, Diving in the crystal clear sea. You can enjoy in the panoramic view of Cetina driving betwen hills using ZIP-LINE or just regular Climbing or Cycling above our apartments.

Boat rides

You can book a excursion or boat and visit one of the mose beautiful places on the world like Zlatni rat Beach, Hvar island, Boat ride to the Brač island or Vrulja beach.

Town next to our place

From Medići - Mimice you can visit amazing places under 60 minutes driving. One of the most famous places to visit is Split, Salona or Trogir, driving to Vranjača caves in Dugopolje next to the exit from the highway. Our neighbors like Omiš, Brela and Makarska is aprox. 10 minutes driving. If you decide to go and feel the nature you can visit NP Krka in Šibenik, Ethno village Kokorići - Vrgorac, Blue and Red lakes at Imotski. In the case that you decide for day trip you can always visit one the most famous towns Dubrovnik.

Shuttle service

You need to help to reach our place with taxi. Feel free to contact us and we are going to make a reservation for taxi pick-up. Price of shuttle service depends from the location where driver is going to meet you.
